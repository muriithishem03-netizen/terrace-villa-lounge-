About Terrace Wedge

A Place Where You
Feel Right at Home

Terrace Wedge Villa & Lounge is a welcoming hotel in the heart of Kutus, Kirinyaga County. We're proud to offer clean, comfortable accommodation and practical facilities for both work and leisure.

Whether you're here for a business trip, attending an event, or just passing through — we want your stay to be simple, pleasant, and memorable for all the right reasons.

  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the property
  • Complimentary breakfast included with every room stay
  • On-site parking — secure and convenient
  • Friendly, attentive staff available around the clock
  • Fully equipped conference room with AV support
  • In-house restaurant and bar lounge
